About Me

The thing that I really loved about chemistry was solving rigorous problems which required more than just knowledge - they needed a little bit of creativity and inspiration too. I loved the 'aha!' moments that I got, but I really didn't like lab work because I'm pretty clumsy - so I looked around for the right career and really fell in love with coding! It scratches that problem solving itch for me, and I like the idea of a job where you can never stop learning.
